Bio

I am a second-year PhD student at the University of Zurich. I am also an affiliated doctoral student at the ETH AI Center. At the University of Zurich I am fortunate to be advised by Giorgia Ramponi. My research interests lie in the mathematical aspects of Reinforcement Learning, in partiuclar Imitation Learning and Multi-Agent Learning.

Previously, I completed my master's degree in mathematics at the University of Mannheim (with honors), where I was co-advised by Claire Vernade , Leif Döring and Simon Weissmann. In my thesis I worked on (contextual) bandits and importance sampling.
